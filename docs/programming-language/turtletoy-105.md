# Turtletoy

Hand-Coded Art for Plotters
===========================

[Create](./turtle/new/) with [code](./syntax/) or download ready-to-plot [designs](./turtle/browse/love/).

[Start Coding](./turtle/new)
[Learn More](./about)

#### Plotter-Ready Exports

Browse and download crisp, scalable SVG and GCODE files optimized for pen plotters, laser cutters, and CNC machines.

#### JavaScript Turtle Graphics

Create single-line art and generative patterns with simple commands: forward, turn, penup, pendown.

#### Share & Remix

Every turtle is open source. Learn from others and share your creations with a single link.
