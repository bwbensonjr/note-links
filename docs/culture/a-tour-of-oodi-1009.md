# A tour of Oodi

A tour of Oodi
==============

A library reaching for the future

2022-10-26
(last update: 2026-04-09)
/
[blinry](/about/)
/
[CC BY-SA 4.0](/about/#license)
/ [travel](/#travel)

There’s something really special next to Helsinki’s central station: A building called *Oodi*, which describes itself as “an innovative library reaching for the future”. Let’s go on a tour!

![An organic-looking building with both sharp corners and smooth edges, against a gray sky.](/oodi/4fccc6671b254665.jpg "An organic-looking building with both sharp corners and smooth edges, against a gray sky.")

![Entrance of that building, with a lot of glass.](/oodi/34dbf96666113346.jpg "Entrance of that building, with a lot of glass.")

---

The first thing I noticed was this giant screen, which explains that idle hanging out is not only allowed, but encouraged! <3

![Other rules are about respect, comfort and well-being, and about the staff wanting to help.](/oodi/8599bd70f4d23ba6.jpg "Other rules are about respect, comfort and well-being, and about the staff wanting to help.")

---

The bottom floor has a big area full of chess and go boards, and there were in fact a couple of people using them!

![A go board on a dark table.](/oodi/80a48aa829fa52d8.jpg "A go board on a dark table.")

---

There’s a restaurant, and a built-in cinema, which shows classic movies for little money.

![Organically-shaped ceiling over tables.](/oodi/ef9cfe8a9b731241.jpg "Organically-shaped ceiling over tables.")

![Cinema entrance, it's called Kino Regina.](/oodi/911febdb1daa15f0.jpg "Cinema entrance, it's called Kino Regina.")

![Retro-looking posters of mostly Finnish movies.](/oodi/c0b064417e5b77dd.jpg "Retro-looking posters of mostly Finnish movies.")

---

The best staircase ever leads us to the second floor:

![It's shaped in a double helix, and its's all black, covered in Finnish words.](/oodi/62792f4fa023b065.jpg "It's shaped in a double helix, and its's all black, covered in Finnish words.")

![](/oodi/b30d5cd031ef9486.jpg)

---

There we find: professional digital working stations, with high-quality screens!

![Black tables with comfortable office chairs, and monitors that have shades so you'll get less reflections.](/oodi/0c18f9313a6d7558.jpg "Black tables with comfortable office chairs, and monitors that have shades so you'll get less reflections.")

---

There’s a big number of recording studios and sound production studios, all of which you can rent!

![Signs lead to studios 5-8.](/oodi/15cd170863574f3e.jpg "Signs lead to studios 5-8.")

---

Oh, and while we’re at it, why don’t we rent some instruments, as well? There’s full-time staff maintaining them.

![Guitars in a glass showcase.](/oodi/9fb0eb9f2544e71d.jpg "Guitars in a glass showcase.")

---

There’s many small and big group rooms which you can rent, many of them in active use! I see group meetings, students working, and podcast interviews being recorded!

![A meeting room behind a glass wall.](/oodi/2083e50d8cf7fe45.jpg "A meeting room behind a glass wall.")

---

Wanna rent a kitchen and cook with your friends? Sure, you can do that here!

![A kitchen behind glass walls.](/oodi/51c34976368b4806.jpg "A kitchen behind glass walls.")

---

You can also rent game rooms with modern video game consoles and VR gear, along with the games to play in them!

![A room with consoles and VR headsets.](/oodi/df8b34b21153de9e.jpg "A room with consoles and VR headsets.")

---

There’s a makerspace: It has several 3D printers, multiple laser cutters, and engravers. And staff which can help you learn how to use them.

![A making space behind glass windows.](/oodi/96fce0c78f258d6a.jpg "A making space behind glass windows.")

---

There’s also: Sewing machines! Shirt presses! Cutting plotters!

![Bespoke things.](/oodi/ce8417f4ff793c83.jpg "Bespoke things.")

![](/oodi/c5fab0723b07ed13.jpg)

---

At this point, you might ask: “Ok, but where are the books?” Let’s go up to the third floor!

![Winding staircase.](/oodi/3d551f4b3b6c08c6.jpg "Winding staircase.")

---

Here they are! Lots and lots of them, lined up in shelves that don’t go above eye height most people, so it’s easy to find other people! During the day, daylight comes in through organically-shaped holes in the ceiling.

![White bookshelves under a cool organic ceiling.](/oodi/463356200b968b7e.jpg "White bookshelves under a cool organic ceiling.")

---

There’s an integrated cafe. Of course you can read books while drinking your coffee!

![A cafe area with a small living tree besides it.](/oodi/06b9879395755d2c.jpg "A cafe area with a small living tree besides it.")

---

At one end, there’s a children’s space, with lots of cleverly integrated ramps, toys, and a wonderully fluffy room! And a parking area for kid’s strollers.

![Wooden area with stairs, ramps, and small people.](/oodi/5ca0b820852f7b56.jpg "Wooden area with stairs, ramps, and small people.")

![A fluffy room with strangely shaped sitting space.](/oodi/52b6a5266c2ea676.jpg "A fluffy room with strangely shaped sitting space.")

![Kid's strollers parking ahead of a city view.](/oodi/b973cb8e6b8327d5.jpg "Kid's strollers parking ahead of a city view.")

---

And there’s space for sitting and working everywhere (with power outlets)! You can sit, lie down, or chill out however you want!

![Another wooden area, with long, lit tables, and fun seats in the back.](/oodi/e473aa23b730d390.jpg "Another wooden area, with long, lit tables, and fun seats in the back.")

---

There’s robots moving books from and to the storage in the basement! 🤖

![A small robot with a book bucket on top.](/oodi/00bbb3ca92083d41.jpg "A small robot with a book bucket on top.")

---

So yeah, I really love it here! I can barely believe a place like this exists, and I hope there’ll be many more like it in the future! <3

---

Oh I forgot – you can do a [virtual online tour through Oodi](https://www.oodihelsinki.fi/en/visit-oodi-in-vr/) yourself! Try making it through the different levels, all the way to the top!

[![A StreetView-like view of Oodi's entrance.](/oodi/a9da2f1c3be13ffb.jpg "A StreetView-like view of Oodi's entrance.")](https://www.oodihelsinki.fi/en/visit-oodi-in-vr/)
