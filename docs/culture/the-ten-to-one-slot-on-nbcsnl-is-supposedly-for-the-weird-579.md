# The ten-to-one slot on @nbcsnl is supposedly for the "weird" stuff, but sometimes it's insanely, magically weird. It's never fair to compare SNL casts but I do think the mid/late 00's group had the largest collection of creative geniuses. Two of them, @willforte & @jasonsudeikis, star in Potato Chip, co-written with John Solomon, a sketch so special it was commemorated with a mock Playbill created by graphic arts legend @tarabdonnelly. I could talk all day about this one but just watch it. - RS
