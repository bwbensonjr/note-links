# bookofjoe: Live Stream From the Namib Desert

Thursday, October 9, 2025 Live Stream From the Namib Desert A couple weeks ago I happened on this live streaming camera near a water hole in the Namib Desert and it's now my first stop every morning. Namibia is 6 hours ahead of my U.S. Eastern Time Zone so it's really hot there when I visit and almost always there are lots of creatures hydrating.
