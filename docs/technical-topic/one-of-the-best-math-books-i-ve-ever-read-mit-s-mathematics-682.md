MIT's "Mathematics for Computer Science"

Its writing style is brilliant, and it covers everything:

- Linear algebra
- Series
- Logic
- Probability
- Number theory
- Graphs

https://courses.csail.mit.edu/6.042/spring18/mcs.pdf
