# Organic Maps: Offline Hike, Bike, Trails and Navigation

Organic Maps: Offline Hike, Bike, Trails and Navigation
=======================================================

**Organic Maps** is a privacy-focused offline maps & GPS app for hiking, cycling, biking, and driving. Absolutely free. No ads. No tracking. Developed with love by the open-source community and the same people, who created [**MapsWithMe**](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Maps.me "Maps.Me")/**Maps.Me** app. Powered by [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/about "OpenStreetMap") data.

**Organic Maps** is one of the few applications nowadays that supports 100% of features without an active Internet connection. Install Organic Maps, download maps, throw away your SIM card, and go for a weeklong trip on a single battery charge without any byte sent to the network.

> In December 2025, Organic Maps [reached 6M installs](https://organicmaps.app/news/2025-12-31/organic-maps-2025-year-in-review/). [Help us](https://organicmaps.app/donate/) to scale!

Features
--------

Organic Maps is the ultimate companion app for travelers, tourists, hikers, drivers, and cyclists:

* Detailed offline maps with places that don't exist on other maps, thanks to [OpenStreetMap](https://www.openstreetmap.org/about "OpenStreetMap")
* Cycling routes, hiking trails, and walking paths
* Contour lines, elevation profiles, peaks, and slopes
* Turn-by-turn walking, cycling, and car navigation with voice guidance and CarPlay/Android Auto support
* Metro/subway maps
* Fast offline search on the downloaded maps
* Import or export bookmarks in KML/KMZ, GPX, GeoJSON formats
* Dark Mode to protect your eyes
* Countries and regions don't take a lot of space
* Wikipedia articles for popular places
* Free and open-source
