# **Active Inference** - the process theory (or agent architecture) derived from the FEP that explains how organisms act on their environment to make their predicted observations come true. This framework unifies perception, planning, and action under a single imperative: to minimize free energy, which is an upper bound on the log-evidence of the agent's model of the world.
