**[OpenHistoricalMap](https://www.openhistoricalmap.org/)** goes beyond the present, mapping places throughout the world… throughout the ages.  
No community is too small, no story too obscure to add to this map.  
Everyone has something to contribute, including you!

[Go to the map](https://www.openhistoricalmap.org/) • [Learn more…](//wiki.openstreetmap.org/wiki/OpenHistoricalMap/FAQ)

Use

Go to [the main map](https://www.openhistoricalmap.org/) and navigate to a place and time of interest using the time slider. Share the map to spread the word about our project. Explore the many ways you can reuse OpenHistoricalMap in your own storytelling and software projects.

* [Browse the map](/wiki/OpenHistoricalMap/Browsing "OpenHistoricalMap/Browsing")
* [Reuse the data](/wiki/OpenHistoricalMap/Reuse "OpenHistoricalMap/Reuse")

Contribute

To contribute to OpenHistoricalMap, go to [the main map](https://www.openhistoricalmap.org/), zoom in to a place of interest, and click the “Edit” button. If you are familiar with editing OpenStreetMap, the OpenHistoricalMap editing experience is very similar. See **[the tagging guidelines](/wiki/OpenHistoricalMap/Tags "OpenHistoricalMap/Tags")** for information on a few changes we’ve made to accommodate the time dimension. Please report any issues and send us your ideas [on GitHub](https://github.com/OpenHistoricalMap/issues/issues/) or [our forum](https://forum.openhistoricalmap.org/).

* [Improve the map](/wiki/OpenHistoricalMap/Contributing "OpenHistoricalMap/Contributing")
* [Improve the software](/wiki/OpenHistoricalMap/Development "OpenHistoricalMap/Development")
* [Donate](https://openstreetmap.app.neoncrm.com/forms/ohm)
